# SPHR Security Log — Guard Quick-Start Guide

Welcome! This is the app you'll use to log your nightly patrols. It installs on your own
phone and only takes a minute to set up. Follow the steps below.

---

## STEP 1 — Install the app on your phone (one time only)

You'll be sent a link (web address) from your manager. Open it in your phone's browser,
then add it to your home screen so it works like a normal app.

**If you have an ANDROID phone (Samsung, Google, etc.):**
1. Open the link in **Chrome** (or Edge).
2. Tap the **⋮ menu** (three dots, top-right).
3. Tap **"Add to Home screen"** (or **"Install app"**).
4. Tap **Add / Install**.
5. The **SPHR** icon now appears on your home screen. Use that from now on.

**If you have an iPhone:**
1. Open the link in **Safari** (this step must be done in Safari).
2. Tap the **Share** button (the square with an arrow pointing up).
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Tap **Add** (top-right).
5. The **SPHR** icon now appears on your home screen. Use that from now on.

> ✅ Tip: Always open the app from the **home-screen icon**, not the browser — it runs
> full-screen and works better that way.

---

## STEP 2 — Allow permissions when asked

The first time you use the camera or record your location, your phone will ask permission.
Tap **Allow**. The app only asks when you actually use those features.
- **Location** — needed to record where each patrol check happened (GPS).
- **Camera** — needed to attach photos to a patrol entry.
- **Microphone** — needed for the voice-to-text notes.

If you accidentally tapped "Don't allow", you can turn it back on in your phone's
**Settings → (the SPHR app) → Permissions**.

---

## STEP 3 — Start your shift

1. Open the **SPHR** app.
2. Enter your **Security Number** and **Guard Name**.
3. Check the **Shift Date** (tap the calendar to change it if needed).
4. Select the **manager on duty** for tonight from the list.
5. Tap **Start Shift**.

> If the app says it found an **unfinished shift**, it will ask whether to **Resume** it or
> **Start New Shift**. If it's from a previous night, choose **Start New Shift**.

---

## STEP 4 — Log a patrol (do this at each check)

1. Tap **Add Entry** (or the + button).
2. Enter the **Location** (where you are) and the **Action** (what you did/checked).
3. The **time** and your **GPS location** are recorded automatically.
4. (Optional) Tap the **camera** to take a photo.
5. (Optional) Use the **microphone** to speak your notes instead of typing.
6. Tap **Save**.

Repeat this for every check point during your patrol. Everything is saved on your phone
**even if you have no signal** — it will send automatically once you're back in range.

---

## STEP 5 — Complete the Final Door Checks (end of shift)

1. Go to the **Door Checks** screen.
2. Tick off each of the 18 doors/gates/areas as you secure them
   (GM Office, Back Door, Pool Gates, Keg Room, Sheds, etc.).
3. You must complete these before you can submit the report.

---

## STEP 6 — Submit your report

1. Go to the **Summary** screen.
2. Review your entries and door checks.
3. Tap **Submit**.
4. You'll see a confirmation that the report has been **emailed to management**.

That's it — the report (with your photos, GPS, times and door checks) is automatically
emailed to management as a PDF. Your shift then clears, ready for the next guard/night.

---

## Quick tips & troubleshooting

- **Open from the home-screen icon**, not the browser.
- **No signal?** Keep logging — it saves on the phone and sends later automatically.
- **App feels slow to open the first time at night?** Give it up to a minute — it "wakes
  up" on the first open, then it's fast.
- **"Check connection" message when submitting?** Make sure you have signal, then try
  again. If it keeps happening, tell your manager.
- **Photo won't attach?** Make sure you tapped **Allow** for camera access
  (Settings → SPHR app → Permissions).

---

### Your nightly routine in 6 words:
**Start shift → Log patrols → Door checks → Submit.**

Thank you for keeping the resort secure. 🔐
